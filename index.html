Co-Design of Large Model Pruning and Dataow for Sparse Tensor Accelerators Since Apr. 2023
Yuhan Qin, Yulong Meng, Haitao Du, Song Chen, Yi Kang Submitted to DATE2024
The model-driven approach to co-optimize the large model pruning strategies and dataow (tiling&scheduling) design.
Co-optimizing the large model pruning strategy alongside the tiling method for better structural sparsity in matrix tiles.

accelerators, subsequently resolving the optimal ne-grained tiling and scheduling strategy.

Dynamic Load Balancing Strategy for SpMV in Process-in-Memory System Since Nov. 2022
Shiyu Fan, Yuhan Qin, Yi Kang (to be determined) In preparation
The dynamic load balancing strategy for SpMV within the framework of a process-near-DRAM-bank system.
Recognized the static and lock-step merge phase of SpMV as the primary source of high synchronization cost.
Utilized a scoreboard to issue merge tasks out-of-order based on their interdependencies and resource requirements.
Contributed to the system-level simulator development based on Ramulator (DRAM) and Booksim (Network-on-Chip).

Exploring Alternatives Architectures for DRAM Bus Oct. 2021 Jul. 2022
Haitao Du, Yuhan Qin, Junkaixuan Li, Song Chen, Yi Kang In preparation
The exploration for multi-hop network as the DRAM bus alternative for bank connection and message transmission.
Modeled further DRAM bus based on ITRS15 and revealed it as the DRAMs energy&parallelism bottleneck.
Advocating for a scoreboard at the memory controller side for out-of-order requests scheduling: simplify the circuit
